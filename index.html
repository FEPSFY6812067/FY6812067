Introduction

     Robotic surgery or else called robot-assisted surgery is a kind of minimally invasive surgery where doctors can execute complex surgeries with the help of robotic devices. This technology was first developed in the mid 1980s, where the first use of robotic surgical arm was to perform a biopsy in 1985 (Lanfranco et al., 2004). Since then, this technology has developed in a more sophisticated and versatile system for numerous different surgical procedures. The aim of this technology is to improve surgical procedures and the outcomes for the patients. It improves precision, flexibility, and control during the operations and gives them a greater view of the site compared with the traditional technique. Currently, there is more than one robotic surgical system however, the most know specialized suite of technologies is called the ‘da VinciTM’
